Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

QuranIQ ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

2. Data We Collect

We believe in "Sacred Minimalism" for data too. We collect only what is strictly necessary for the app to function:

  • Account Information: Name and Email address (for authentication and saving your progress).
  • Usage Data: In-app search queries and journey progress (stored to personalize your experience).
  • Technical Data: Minimal device information required for app stability and crash reporting.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the App service.
  • Save your "Journeys" and "Saved Inquiries".
  • Improve the accuracy of our AI-assisted search results.

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Religious Content & AI Disclaimer

QuranIQ uses Artificial Intelligence to synthesize responses based on Islamic texts. While we strive for accuracy by sourcing from trusted APIs (like Al-Quran Cloud), AI responses may occasionally be imprecise. Please consult qualified scholars for specific religious rulings (Fatwas).
